Hopefully these examples illustrate to you the power of this grid system. It's a bit trickier to get your head around than most but it is worth it in my opinion because this way, we can let the grid classes deal with all grid related things. We don't need to apply margins or paddings on other stuff to align it. We can just focus on those individual components.
