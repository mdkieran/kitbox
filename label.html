Pointing

The pointers on the labels are pseudo elements and do not alter the positioning of the labels in any way. If you want some margin then you can use kb_grid mar_left or mar_right.

Label Label Label Label

Corners

Corner labels look for the nearest relatively positioned element and then stick to the top left or right of it. They are linkable too.
